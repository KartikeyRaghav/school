Director Academics' Message

Dr. Madhu Juneja
Director Academics SDAV

“Excellence is a continuous process and not an accident.”- Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam
A school plays a central role in developing and nurturing the latent talents of students and shaping of their characters. In this fast changing education system and technology, we have to prepare the students to face new challenges and uncertainty in life .The present and future plans of learning are redefining the contours of teaching and learning.
Our school concerns the holistic development and well-being of every learner and we foster in them the 21st century adequate life skills like, critical thinking, creativity, empathy, decision making and communication. Our constant endeavour is to identify students' interests at the initial stage and support them to build on their talents. They are motivated to explore new frontiers of learning and are prepared to connect with real-world situations through experiential learning .Our transition to virtual school in the wake of pandemic has brought forth new dimensions to teaching and learning and the several benefits that collaborative work in the digital environment offers .
Our dedicated and committed team of teachers, enlightened by the Management of dynamic educationists, is our building block. The team motivates and inspires the students to believe in themselves and provides the excellent education that will make them successful good human being. We strive hard to make the best possible efforts to inculcate strong values combined with academics highlighting the enquiry based learning and personalised instructions as the key pattern, along with various intra and inter school co- curricular activities.
I feel honoured and privileged to be a part of this educational institution where we work, learn and grow together. We set our target, know our target and always keep it at the forefront of our mind.
