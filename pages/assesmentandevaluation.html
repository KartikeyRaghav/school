Assesment and Evaluation

Evaluation is the best way to inculcate the habit of regular and systematic work amongst the students. It is a device to evaluate and assess the students in a holistic manner.

We undertake two internal evaluations per academic year, as per CBSE rules. The question papers are divided into sections according to the syllabus. Final Term Exams are held in February/March for grades I-IX and XI, with results announced in the third week of March. Pre-Board exams for classes X and XII are held in the months of December and January.

Each Term concludes with the distribution of a report card.
