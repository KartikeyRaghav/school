History of the school

In January. 1970, on the pressing demand and genuine need of the citizens of Gurugram, Shri Sanatan Dharam Adarsh Vidyalaya Society, Gurugram was setup under the auspices of Smt. Bhagwati Vasishtha, educationist and an eminent lawyer, Pt. Hari Ram Shastri and Pt. BasantLal Sharma were pioneers whose untiring efforts contributed immensely. Today the school is being run by an efficient management under the presidentship of a dynamic educationist Smt. Asha Sharma. Under her expertise, the school has grown by leaps and bounds. She has successfully set up a new building in the spacious, sylvan surroundings on Sohna Road, Gurugram.

Educational Goals

S.D. Adarsh Vidyalaya is a co-educational English Medium Senior Secondary School. One of the objectives of the institution is to initiate the students to the rich cultural heritage of our county and to provide the basic concept of the religion. Besides inculcating patriotic spirit and grooming children into worthy citizens, the school also develops a cosmopolitan outlook. The physical, cultural and intellectual developments of the children receive utmost care. The school attaches great importance to various scholastic and co-scholastic activities besides the class routine. These activities are designed to develop valuable traits of character such as self-confidence, diligence and competitive spirit among the students.
